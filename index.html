4-Day Upper/Lower Split

Warm-Up & Mobility

- 5 min cardio
- Arm circles 10
- Leg swings 10
- Hip circles 10
- Cat-Cow 5
- Glute bridges 2×10
- Scapular push-ups 2×10

Day 1 – Push

- Bench 4×6–8
- OHP 3×8–10
- Push-Ups 3×AMRAP
- Dips 3×8–12
- Laterals 3×12–15

Day 2 – Lower

- Squat 4×6–8
- RDL 3×8–10
- Lunges 3×10/leg
- Glute Bridge 3×12–15
- Calf Raise 3×15–20

Day 3 – Pull

- Pull-Ups 4×AMRAP
- Rows 4×8–10
- Incline DB Press 3×8–10
- Face Pull 3×12–15
- Hammer Curl 3×10–12

Day 4 – Lower

- Deadlift 4×4–6
- Squat var 3×8–10
- Split Squat 3×8–10
- Good Morning 3×10–12
- Calf Raise 3×15–20

Progression

Form → Add weight weekly → Deload week 9
